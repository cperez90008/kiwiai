# SKILL: Meeting Notes & Summaries

## When to use
User pastes a meeting transcript or notes and wants it processed.

## Extract
1. **Summary** — 2-3 sentences on what the meeting was about
2. **Key decisions** — What was decided
3. **Action items** — [Person] will [action] by [date]
4. **Open questions** — Unresolved items needing follow-up
5. **Next steps** — What happens next and when

## Format
Clear headers, bullet points, bold names for action item owners.
Flag unassigned action items as [UNASSIGNED].
