# SKILL: Email Manager

## When to use this skill
When the user asks to write, draft, reply to, summarize, or manage emails.

## Drafting emails
- Ask for: recipient, purpose, tone (formal/casual), key points
- Default tone: professional but warm
- Always end with a clear call to action
- Keep subject lines under 60 characters
- Offer 2-3 subject line options for important emails

## Summarizing email threads
- Identify: who sent what, the main issue/ask, decisions made, outstanding action items

## Email types
- Cold outreach
- Follow-up after no response  
- Declining politely
- Requesting a meeting
- Delivering bad news
- Apologizing professionally
- Negotiating terms

## Style guidance
- Mirror the formality level the user describes
- Active voice, short paragraphs
- No filler phrases ("I hope this email finds you well")
