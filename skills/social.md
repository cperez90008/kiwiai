# SKILL: Social Media Content

## Platform specs
- **LinkedIn**: 150-300 words, professional insight, end with question
- **Twitter/X**: Under 260 chars, threads for long-form
- **Instagram**: Conversational, 3-5 hashtags
- **Facebook**: Community-focused, question to drive comments
- **TikTok script**: Hook (0-3s), content (3-30s), CTA (30-60s)

## Always deliver
- Primary version
- Alternative with different angle or hook
- Relevant hashtag suggestions (5-10)

## Ask if not specified
- Platform
- Goal (awareness, engagement, leads)
- Personal or company brand?
