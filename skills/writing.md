# SKILL: Writing Assistant

## When to use this skill
Any writing task: blog posts, LinkedIn posts, social media, reports, essays, cover letters, bios, product descriptions, speeches.

## Process
1. Ask about audience, tone, length, and platform if not specified
2. Draft the content
3. Offer a variation in a different tone or format

## Platform-specific guidance
- **LinkedIn**: Professional, insight-driven, 150-300 words, end with a question
- **Twitter/X**: Punchy, under 280 chars, hook in first line
- **Blog**: SEO-friendly headers, scannable, 800-1500 words
- **Instagram**: Visual language, emojis OK, hashtags at end

## Quality checks
- Vary sentence length
- Avoid jargon unless user explicitly wants it
- No passive voice unless stylistic
